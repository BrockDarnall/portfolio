Virtual Machine

Step 1: Open up the virual machine.


Step 2: Press new and choose a name for your VM.


Step 3: Choose where you want to put your storage.


This is how to create a VM.

VM's are useful because if you crash the VM
its much easier to get back your files than if you crashed the computer.

Step 4: Choose how much storage the VM will take up.


Step 5: Start up the virual machine.
